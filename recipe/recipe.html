Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

A quick and tasty Italian pasta dish perfect for a weeknight meal!

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 2 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until done.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy, then add the garlic and saut√© for a minute.
  3. Whisk the eggs and Parmesan together in a bowl.
  4. Drain the pasta and reserve some cooking water.
  5. Toss the pasta with the pancetta, then remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture.
  6. Add a splash of pasta water if needed to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Season with salt and pepper, and serve with extra Parmesan on top!
