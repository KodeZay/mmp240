About the Show

The Walking Dead is a post-apocalyptic horror series created by Frank Darabont. The story follows a group of survivors navigating a world overrun by zombies, dealing with both the undead and other human threats.

Show Details

Creator: Frank Darabont

Cast: Andrew Lincoln, Norman Reedus, Melissa McBride, Lauren Cohan, Danai Gurira

Genre: Drama, Horror, Thriller
